Part I : Moving Picture

The Gap:
The current NFT ecosystem is heavily revolving around JPEG type of NFTs. As the space is maturing, the next natural evolution will be the adoption of “moving JPEGS”.


The Solution:
Art of Mob will build “ Moving Picture”, an NFT marketplace for video based content. The content can range from GIFs, video clips, all the way to short films, movies and VR experiences. JPEG NFTs have changed the lives of millions of graphic designers, illustrators, animators, painters, photographers, etc.. Art of Mob wants to maximize this spectrum. Content creators, influencers, videographers, filmmakers, gamers, educators, athletes will be all able to directly monetize their work and bring it to the spotlight on this revolutionary marketplace.


Staking:
Moving Picture will be powered by $POP coin which will be earned through staking Art of Mob NFTs. The tokenomics are carefully calculated to form a strong foundation for the Marketplace. The utility for $POP is developed to make the token deflationary and to provide higher value for the holders of Art of Mob NFTs. Whitepaper with the tokenomics to be released soon.


Scalability:
Art of Mob signed partnerships with RobocomVR and Cntrl Movie, highly acclaimed companies in the film and VR industries. These partnerships will enable Art of Mob to populate the marketplace’s library with commercial content. A collectibles feature will be added for users who wish to own scenes and quotes from their favorite movies, shows and characters.
